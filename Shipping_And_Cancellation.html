Shipping and Cancellation

we dont cancel any order after booking the shipment , incase due to some reason you are not able to receive the order for some reason from your side we wont be able to refund shipping , packing and transactional charges of 3% for payment gateway . no urgent orders are undertaken . We take utmost care in shipping of your products. But to make all procedure smooth and easy please allow us 1-3 working days for your order to be dispatched. Normally orders placed before 12 pm on a business day, we aim to ship the same day, providing card security checks are complete, payment received and stock availability is confirmed. Orders placed after 12 pm will be shipped the very next business day. Orders Received on Sunday or during Holidays are dispatched the following Monday or next working day. During busy times, such as holiday periods, there could be a long processing time and shipping delays so please bear with us. If in case your Pin code is accessible only through India Speed post , you must include it in Notes so that we will ship through it to . Rural domestic addresses require one or more additional days to deliver.

ExtroKids uses delhivery , India post and all leading courier companies for the shipment of your products and generally the time taken to dispatch any product anywhere in India is estimated as 48 hrs to 72 hrs. However if the product is bulky the parcel will be shipped by surface (by road) which would take 7 -10 days to reach until requested by the customer for an urgent delivery or by air. At Extrokids, we understand the concerns faced by modern parents about their children's cognitive development amidst the digital age. Our journey began with a deep understanding of the challenges parents face in balancing screen time with enriching activities. As a mother, I have personally witnessed the transformative power of engaging children in activities that stimulate their minds and foster holistic development.

With this insight, our primary focus lies in nurturing the cognitive skills of children to empower them to excel in both education and career. We meticulously curate our collection of toys, books, and craft supplies to provide avenues for children to explore, learn, and grow outside the confines of screens. Each product is carefully chosen to ignite curiosity, spark creativity, and promote critical thinking skills, laying a strong foundation for their future success.

Our commitment goes beyond merely offering products; we strive to build a community of like-minded parents dedicated to providing their children with the best opportunities for growth and development. Through Extrokids, we aim to create a supportive environment where parents can find inspiration, guidance, and resources to nurture their child's cognitive abilities and unlock their full potential.

Welcome to Extrokids Online Store, where every purchase is not just a transaction but a step towards building a brighter future for our children. Together, let's embark on this journey of exploration, learning, and growth, one toy, one book, and one craft at a time.

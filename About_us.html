ABOUT US

Welcome to Extrokids Online Store, a heartfelt initiative born from the vision of a mother of two with a mission to touch the lives of millions of fellow mommies and enhance the cognitive development of their children. In light of recent research showing the adverse effects of excessive screen time, such as ADHD, autism, and developmental delays, we are passionately committed to providing sustainable solutions to keep kids engaged in meaningful activities for extended periods.

At Extrokids, we understand the concerns faced by modern parents about their children's cognitive development amidst the digital age. Our journey began with a deep understanding of the challenges parents face in balancing screen time with enriching activities. As a mother, I have personally witnessed the transformative power of engaging children in activities that stimulate their minds and foster holistic development.

With this insight, our primary focus lies in nurturing the cognitive skills of children to empower them to excel in both education and career. We meticulously curate our collection of toys, books, and craft supplies to provide avenues for children to explore, learn, and grow outside the confines of screens. Each product is carefully chosen to ignite curiosity, spark creativity, and promote critical thinking skills, laying a strong foundation for their future success.

Our commitment goes beyond merely offering products; we strive to build a community of like-minded parents dedicated to providing their children with the best opportunities for growth and development. Through Extrokids, we aim to create a supportive environment where parents can find inspiration, guidance, and resources to nurture their child's cognitive abilities and unlock their full potential.

Welcome to Extrokids Online Store, where every purchase is not just a transaction but a step towards building a brighter future for our children. Together, let's embark on this journey of exploration, learning, and growth, one toy, one book, and one craft at a time.
